Diving Areas

Explore Our Diving sites

Dive into paradise beneath Nilaveli's waves:

Imagine plunging into crystal-clear water, vibrant coral reefs teeming with life unfolding before you. Dive into Nilaveli Beach, Sri Lanka, and witness a kaleidoscope of colors.

Glide alongside graceful butterflyfish, encounter playful clownfish in anemones, and maybe even spot majestic reef sharks cruising nearby. The renowned Pigeon Island, teeming with marine life, is a short boat ride away, offering dives for all levels. Whether you're a seasoned diver or a curious beginner, Nilaveli's warm waters and diverse underwater landscapes promise an unforgettable adventure. So, take the plunge and discover a world of wonder beneath the waves!

Where history meets the deep: Diving in Trincomalee

Beneath the golden sands of Trincomalee Beach lies a hidden realm pulsating with life.

Immerse yourself in turquoise waters and explore vibrant coral gardens teeming with exotic fish. Dive past ancient shipwrecks, remnants of a rich maritime past, and encounter playful dolphins or even majestic manta rays. Head to Swami Rock, where moray eels peek from crevices, or explore the Fort Frederick Point teeming with colorful reef dwellers. Whether you're a seasoned adventurer or a curious novice, Trincomalee's underwater world offers an unforgettable blend of history, mystery, and marine magic. So, gear up and discover the wonders waiting in the deep blue!

Diving in Mirissa Beach Sri Lanka

Mirissa Beach beckons divers with a vibrant underwater world just beneath the golden sands. Reefs teeming with life await exploration, from playful schools of butterflyfish to elusive Napoleon wrasse.

Beginners can explore shallow dive sites like Lopsta Point, while more experienced divers can venture deeper to Godagala, spotting turtles and vibrant corals. Witness manta rays dancing in the plankton-rich waters, or encounter reef sharks patrolling the depths. Whether you seek underwater serenity or heart-pounding encounters, Mirissa's diverse dive sites offer unforgettable memories for every kind of diver.

Dive into History and Coral Delights in Unawatuna:

Unawatuna Beach offers a unique blend of historical and underwater wonders. Glide past colorful reefs alive with butterflyfish and clownfish, or delve deeper to explore historic wrecks like the famed "Rangoon," teeming with marine life.

For the adventurous, shark dives offer adrenaline-pumping encounters. Between dives, relax on the pristine beach, or wander through the charming village, steeped in Dutch colonial history. Unawatuna caters to all diving levels, whether you're a first-timer marveling at your first coral garden or a seasoned adventurer seeking historical depths. Dive into Unawatuna and discover a world where vibrant corals meet centuries-old stories.

Hikkaduwa: Dive into a kaleidoscope of coral and calm:

Hikkaduwa Beach entices divers with a vibrant underwater playground. Gentle currents weave through colorful coral gardens, bustling with butterflyfish, angelfish, and even playful turtles.

Beginners can take their first breaths underwater in shallow reefs like Gunjanastana, while veterans can explore deeper sites like the wreck of the "SS Conch" or drift alongside majestic reef sharks at "Bentota Rock." Between dives, relax on the golden sands, soak up the vibrant surfer vibe, or explore the local shops selling vibrant handicrafts. Whether you're a wide-eyed newbie or a seasoned explorer, Hikkaduwa's warm waters and diverse marine life promise an unforgettable dive adventure.
